Welcome to LXNDRIA

LXNDRIA is a community-driven platform for problem-solving and discussions. Whether you love tackling complex challenges or engaging in thoughtful discussions, this space is designed for you.
If you're new here, we encourage you to explore, contribute, and become part of our growing community!

Meet the Creator

LXNDRIA was created by a passionate developer dedicated to fostering a space where knowledge-sharing thrives. Built as an open-source project, it welcomes contributions from like-minded individuals who value meaningful engagement.

The Vision Behind LXNDRIA

The inspiration behind LXNDRIA stems from the desire to create a space where users can collaboratively explore complex problems, discuss ideas, and challenge themselves. As a former user of (now discontinued) Brilliant.org’s community, I was inspired by its interactive problem-solving approach. When its discussion features were discontinued, I felt the need to create a space where like-minded individuals could continue engaging in meaningful challenges. Unlike traditional course-based learning platforms, LXNDRIA focuses on interactive problem-solving and open-ended discussions, ensuring a dynamic and evolving knowledge base for all participants.

Getting Started & Community Principles

LXNDRIA is designed for thoughtful discussions and collaborative learning. Here are some key guidelines:

  • Engage Respectfully - No personal attacks or hate speech.
  • Stay on Topic - Keep discussions STEM-related.
  • No Self-Promotion - Advertising or marketing is not allowed.
  • No Explicit Content - Any form of inappropriate content is prohibited.
  • Report Violations - Help maintain a high-quality community.

How to Format Your Posts

LXNDRIA supports Markdown for formatting your posts, allowing you to create well-structured and readable discussions. Here are some key features you can use:

  • **Bold** → Bold
  • *Italic* → Italic
  • `Inline Code` → Inline Code

Code Blocks

To add code blocks, use triple backticks:
like this:
```
print("Hello, LXNDRIA!")
```
which will display as: 

            
print("Hello, LXNDRIA!")

Posting Images

To add images in Markdown:

            
![Alt Text](image-url)

You can use resources like postimages.org

Adding Links

Use the following syntax to add links:

            
[Descriptive Link Text](https://example.com)

Math Support

LXNDRIA supports LaTeX, you can use:

            
\(E=mc^2\) → \(E=mc^2\)

For inline math, and:

            
\[
\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
\]

For centered math display

Join Our Moderation Team

LXNDRIA is maintained by a team of dedicated moderators who ensure that discussions remain constructive and respectful. If you're interested in becoming a moderator, you can apply by submitting an application that includes:

  • Your background and experience with community moderation.
  • Why you're interested in the role.
  • How you would contribute to upholding LXNDRIA’s values.
Applications are reviewed periodically, and selected moderators will be onboarded with necessary guidelines.

Your Questions, Answered

Is LXNDRIA free to use?

Yes! LXNDRIA is completely free and open to all users.

Can I contribute to LXNDRIA’s development?

Absolutely! LXNDRIA is open-source, and we welcome contributions. You can check out our GitHub repository to get started.

How do I report inappropriate content?

You can report any violations using the "Report" button on posts or by contacting the moderation team directly - via mail or you can tag them in the post.

What kind of topics are allowed?

LXNDRIA focuses on STEM-related topics, including mathematics, physics, computer science, and more. If you're unsure about a topic, feel free to ask the community or moderators for guidance.

Is LXNDRIA a replacement for Brilliant.org?

Not exactly. While LXNDRIA shares a similar spirit of problem-solving and discussion, it is an independent, open-source initiative with a focus on community-driven engagement.
